How to rewire your brain for mobile first

Site point
  • Mobile first development leads to focus on content and core functionality.
  • Going the other way leads us to have problems with transfering to mobile devices.

How to improve UX by understanding users

    Be there. Be useful. Be quick. Things are easier to reach at the bottom. Traffic and revenue stay longer at faster speeds. Reduce images to SVG Optimize and minify css and js files Gzip files to eliminate unnessisary data.

Better Mobile UX with strategy menu design

    Most commonly used menu items should be at the bottom. Icons are not always understood. Make it clear what elements are.

Mobile friendly forms

    Keep forms simple. Ditch labels, put them in the field instead. Progress indicate if there is a lot of pagination. Allow autocomplete. Allow view password by default.Allow to hide. Bring up the appropriate keyboard.

Advertising experience

    Do not use pop ups. Do not let ads block content. Content first. Keep them small and simple. Video ads result in higher conversion rates.
